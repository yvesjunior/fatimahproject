
Fatimah Project Mission is a Christian non-profit organization whose mission is to empower and evangelize women and girls through housing, education, and community-driven aid.

Our vision is to bring Christ, education, and empowerment to every woman.

Our vision is that all women across the world experience a fulfilled life in Jesus Christ

.We want to find in those whom we serve, in Jesus’ name, the abundance and fulfillment of a life filled with gratitude and salvation.

This is WHO we are.

Fatimah Project Mission (FPM), formally known as Hail Mary Care Outreach (HMCO) is a Nonprofit Organization registered in the State of Texas. Our mission is to empower and evangelize women and girls through housing, education, and community-driven aid.

Education and Evangelization at FPM are grounded on the truth of the gospel, awareness of cultural sensitivities, and cultivating trust through transparent, competent, and on-time service provisions to community members.
